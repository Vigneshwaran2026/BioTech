DNA Extraction from Mammalian Cells
Author: Dr. Sarah Chen Version: 2.3 (Mar 15, 2026) Duration: 2 hours
1
Cell Harvesting

Remove growth medium and wash cells with PBS. Add trypsin-EDTA and incubate at 37°C for 3-5 minutes.

2
Cell Lysis

Add lysis buffer and incubate at room temperature for 10 minutes. Vortex gently every 2 minutes.

3
DNA Precipitation

Add ice-cold ethanol and incubate at -20°C for 30 minutes. Centrifuge at 12,000g for 10 minutes.
